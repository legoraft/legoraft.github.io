About me

Hi! I'm Legoraft, I love Linux and making things by myself. I'm always working on some form of project and it almost always involves Linux customization or my Minecraft server.

I have a tiny Minecraft survival server on which I play with a friend of mine, I love building Minecraft farms and large redstone contraptions, while keeping them pretty. The friend I play with is an amazing builder, which adds to all the prettiness on our server.

I've used Linux for about a year now and I love hacking around in my Arch Linux installation to try and make the best custom linux OS I can design. I'm still messing with my awesomewm config to make it as beautiful and easy to use as possible.

I love to make a lot of stuff, so take a look around on my blog to read up on my projects or check out my Github where a lot of my projects have their own repositories.
